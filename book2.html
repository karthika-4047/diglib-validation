Death of Innocence - A psychological murder mystery

Haranath Ghosh



It is a psychological murder mystery which delves upon the latent characteristics of human behaviour. No one is all good or all bad. Our personas are defined by the traits we let out to the outer world (or how well we hide the dark ones).

This murder mystery takes readers through a crisscrossed love journey of two young couples Riya & Sagar and Jasmeen & Mridul.

Sagar Chakraborty & Riya Sharma are from two different worlds, but they go against their families to get married. They have their share of initial wedding woes due to the differences in their family backgrounds. Riya's younger sister, Sneha, a 16-year-old fun loving bold girl goes missing from her bungalow in Gurgaon. The local police round up her family members, friends, and teachers but there is no progress on the case.

A special investigator, Mridul Munet from Delhi Police Special Cell is assigned on the case, who takes help from his psychologist ex-girlfriend Jasmeen Sandhu to decode the characters and crack the case.
